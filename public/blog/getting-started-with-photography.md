# Getting Started with Photography

Photography is the art of capturing light and moments in time. Whether you're using a smartphone or a professional DSLR camera, the principles of good photography remain the same.

## Why Start Photography?

Photography offers a unique way to see the world. It encourages you to:
- Explore new places
- Notice details you might otherwise miss
- Express your creativity
- Share your perspective with others

## Basic Equipment

You don't need expensive equipment to start. A smartphone camera is more than capable of taking stunning photos. As you progress, you might consider investing in:

- A mirrorless or DSLR camera
- A versatile lens (18-55mm is a great starting point)
- A tripod for stability
- A camera bag for protection

## Camera Settings to Master

The three pillars of photography are:

1. **Aperture** - Controls depth of field and light
2. **Shutter Speed** - Determines motion blur
3. **ISO** - Adjusts sensor sensitivity to light
